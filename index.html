Questions Answered

In most cases a technician is dispatched within 2 hours of a confirmed booking during normal hours (8 AM – 8 PM). For emergency requests, we offer priority slots that can have a qualified engineer at your door within 60–90 minutes, subject to technician availability in your pin code area.
All replacement parts and labour performed during your service visit are covered for 90 days from the date of repair. If the same problem recurs within this period, we will re-attend and resolve it at zero additional cost. The warranty is automatically registered against your phone number at the time of service.
Always. Our technician first performs a diagnostic (free for most service types), then presents a full itemised quote covering parts and labour before touching anything. You are never charged more than the agreed amount. If you choose not to proceed after the diagnostic, you only pay the small visit fee.
Our engineers carry a comprehensive stock of the most commonly needed parts for popular appliance brands. For less common or brand-specific OEM components, we typically source and return within 24–48 hours at no extra visit charge. When you upload a photo at booking time, we pre-identify likely parts and load the van accordingly.
We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Godrej, Haier, Voltas, Daikin, Carrier, Blue Star, Hitachi, IFB, Bosch, Siemens, Lloyd, and many more. If you are unsure whether your specific model is covered, message us on WhatsApp with the model number and we will confirm within minutes.
Yes. You can reschedule your slot up to 2 hours before the confirmed appointment time at no charge. Cancellations made with at least 2 hours' notice incur no fee. Late cancellations (under 2 hours) may be subject to a small visit fee of ₹99 to partially cover the technician's travel costs.
Every technician on the CoolComfort network is background-checked (police verification), Aadhaar-verified, and holds a valid service certification for the appliance categories they work on. CoolComfort also carries comprehensive public liability insurance covering all on-site visits. You can view a technician's profile and rating before your appointment.
We accept all major payment methods: UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash. Payment is collected only after the service is completed and you are satisfied with the work. No advance payment is required at the time of booking.
